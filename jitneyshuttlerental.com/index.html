About Us

Jitney Shuttle Rental provides premium group transportation across South Jersey, Philadelphia, and Delaware. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate outing, wine tour, or something else, we ensure you arrive safely and on time.

Locally owned and operated, we pride ourselves on attentive customer service and a commitment to making every trip memorable. Our jitneys have a wheelchair accessible entrance.
